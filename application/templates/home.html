Key Parameters for Car Price Prediction

  • Aspiration: Standard (std), Turbo (turbo)
  • Car Body: Convertible, Hatchback, Sedan, Wagon, Hardtop
  • Drive Wheel: Rear-wheel drive (rwd), Front-wheel drive (fwd), 4-wheel drive (4wd)
  • Engine Type: Double Overhead Camshaft (dohc), Overhead Camshaft, V-shaped (ohcv), Overhead Camshaft (ohc), Lateral (l), Rotary (rotor), Overhead Camshaft Front (ohcf)
  • Cylinder Number: Two, Four, Five, Six, Eight
  • Fuel Economy: (citympg x 0.55) + (highwaympg x 0.45)
    • CityMPG: City miles per gallon
    • HighwayMPG: Highway miles per gallon
  • Car Length (Inches): Length of car, Measured in inches
  • Car Width (Inches): Width of car, Measured in inches
  • Curb Weight(Pounds): Weight of a car without occupants or baggage, Measured in pounds
  • Enginesize (CID): Size of engine, measured in Cubic Inches (CID)
  • Bore Ratio: Represents how many times the bore diameter is in relation to the stroke length within the engine cylinder
  • Horsepower (HP): Power of car engine, Measured in HP
  • Wheelbase: Distance between front and rear wheel, Measured in inches
  • Car Price Range: Price range classifier based on the average prices of the cars of each car company
    • Budget Price: Chevrolet, Dodge, Honda, Isuzu, Mitsubishi, Plymouth, Renault, Subaru, Toyota
    • Medium Price: Alfa-Romeo, Audi, Mazda, Mercury, Nissan, Peugeot, Saab, Volkswagen, Volvo
    • High Price: Jaguar, Buick, Porsche
